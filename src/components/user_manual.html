OpenParking: User Manual

Introduction

The population on Earth continues to grow while available space for them has not kept up in pace. Overpopulation of
cities combined with the lack of real estate development for parking space has created a parking crisis.
OpenParking app allows property owners to rent their parking spot at their driveways to users for a period of time.
Property owners make money by renting unused space while users can choose from affordable options in comparison to
public parking. OpenParking focuses on the convenience of users to utilize parking spaces and contributors earn money
renting their vacant spots. By creating a practical, profitable solution, we hope to utilize unused private parking spaces
and diffuse the current parking crisis.


Installation Instructions

    To install OpenParking, you can download the .apk from our github repository at:
    https://github.com/open-parking/OpenParking.

    The .apk is located in:
    https://github.com/open-parking/OpenParking/blob/master/OpenParking%20Code/app/debug/OpenParking.apk.

    Launch OpenParking.apk on your Android phone, and accept the install confirmation. After that, you should be able to
    find the OpenParking app within your app directory. You may need to update your Google Play Services in order to use
    our application.


App Description

    Main Features:

    1. User Sign-up:
      1. Create an account by entering a name, email, and password. You will be asked to verify your account by
        accessing a link sent to your email address. Once you verify the account, you will be able to login.
    2. Welcome Page:
      1. Buy: Need parking? Find some parking by looking at parking spaces near you on the Map or using the List.
      2. Sell: Want to sell parking? Enter your driveway details to get started.
      3. Account: Look up your account details and information on this page.
      4. My Listings: Look up and manage the parking spaces you registered.
      5. My Rating: View your community rating.
    3. Buy
      1. Map: View parking spaces near you on a map! You can scroll on the map, zoom in and out, search for parking
        spaces in a area by zipcode, and select the parking space for more information. To search, enter a zip
        code in the text bar at the top of the app and then select the search button (the magnifying glasses).
      2. List: Browse parking spaces with a list! This is a infinte scrolling list of parking spaces that, when you
        reach the end, takes you back ro the top of the list automatically. You can select a list entry to find
        out more detail about the parking space.
    4. Sell
      1. Create your own parking space and start earning money! In order to create a listing, you will need to enter
        your address, zipcode, time when it is available, and the time when it is unavailable. The latitude and
        longitude will be generated automatically when you enter your address. Once you�ve made sure that your
        information is correct, you can press �Confirm� to create the listing for others to see.
    5. Account
      1. View your account information and also what other users can see of you here.
    6. My Listings
      1. View and manage your current parking space listings. If you do not have any parking space listings, nothing
        will be shown here. You can select your parking instances and view their details.
    7. My Rating
      1. View your community rating. Your rating is decided by reviews users leave when they rate your parking space
        after using it. To raise it, try making your parking space friendlier or easier to use. If your rating
        is too low, users may not want to use your parking space, so be careful and respectful!
